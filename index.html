Here are your next steps to becoming the newest SNHU campus student:

Summer orientation is a unique opportunity to meet your future classmates. You'll also learn about campus living, services for commuter students, financial aid, campus jobs, academic advising and ways to get involved on campus.

  1. Log in to our Residence Life Portal using your MySNHU email and password.
  2. Click on "Access MyHousing Director" at the top of the page.
  3. Click on "Applications and Housing Agreements" on the banner when you reach your MyHousing home page. A drop-down menu will then appear.
  4. Select “2025-2026 Housing License – Incoming Student Application” from the list.

If you can’t find your Housing License Agreement, please email housingquestions@snhu.edu for assistance.

Visit the Patient Portal and complete the required online forms by our June 30 deadline.

Complete the 2025-2026 Health Insurance Waiver on the Anthem website by our August XX deadline.

You can access your tuition bill from the Student Account Center when you visit the mySNHU portal.  

To learn about available payment options, please contact our Student Financial Services (SFS) team at 603.645.9640 or sfscampus@snhu.edu. Our SFS team can help you pay your bill and/or enroll in an interest-free payment plan. Any approved payment plans will be made available to you in your Student Account Center once your bill is finalized.

Visit the Permit Store Website and click on “Order Permits” to get started.

Go to the SNHU Campus Bookstore website and enter your course information to find out which textbook(s) and other course materials you may need for the upcoming term.

While you’re free to choose your own computer for college, you should ensure it meets the “Requirements for Campus Students” listed on our Technical Requirements page. (Please scroll to the bottom.)  

If you have any questions, please contact our IT Service Desk at 1.855.877.9919.
